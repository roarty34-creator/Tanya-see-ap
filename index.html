Tanya Deep Sea Fishing App • Boat Guide
Boat Safety Notes
Check wind + swell before launch
Good wind: under 15 km/h = comfortable, 15–25 km/h = workable, above 25 km/h = rougher, above 30 km/h = poor boat day.

Good swell: under 1.5 m = comfortable, 1.5–2.5 m = workable, above 2.5 m = rough, above 3 m = usually poor for bottom fishing.

If current is too strong, drift may be too fast for bottom fishing — how to know
You will struggle to hold bottom, your line will angle away fast, sinkers won’t stay down properly, and the boat will drift over the reef too quickly before your bait stays in the strike zone.
