Tanya Deep Sea Garmin Edition • Weather
Check wind and swell before launch.

Good wind: under 15 km/h comfortable, 15–25 km/h workable, above 25 km/h rougher, above 30 km/h poor boat day.

Good swell: under 1.5 m comfortable, 1.5–2.5 m workable, above 2.5 m rough, above 3 m usually poor for bottom fishing.

If current is too strong, drift may be too fast for bottom fishing. Your line will angle away fast and sinkers may not hold bottom properly.
