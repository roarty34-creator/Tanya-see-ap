Tanya Deep Sea Fishing App • Species Season Guide
Cape Kob — Best from spring to autumn. Stronger on warmer water, lower light, pushing tide, reef edges and sandy channels.

Yellowtail — Best from summer to autumn. Best when warm water, birds and bait balls are active.

Bonito — Best from summer to autumn. Usually better with warm water and fast surface feeding action.

Red Roman — Good all year. More dependent on reef structure and correct bottom bait presentation.

Snapper — Good all year. More dependent on reef, depth, current and bottom bait than calendar alone.

Silverfish — Good all year. Usually better over reef in calmer workable conditions.

Hottentot — Good all year. Common reef fish where positioning and bait matter more than season.

Yellow Belly — Good all year. Focus on reef patches, current and proper bait on the bottom.

Red Steenbras — Closed season: 1 Sep - 30 Nov. Sensitive species. Handle carefully.
Tanya Deep Sea Fishing App • Rules & Limits
Bag limit: check current permit.

Mixed species total: check current permit.

Check wind and swell before launch.

Good wind: under 15 km/h comfortable, 15–25 km/h workable, above 25 km/h rougher, above 30 km/h poor boat day.

Good swell: under 1.5 m comfortable, 1.5–2.5 m workable, above 2.5 m rough, above 3 m usually poor for bottom fishing.

If current is too strong, drift may be too fast for bottom fishing. Your line will angle away fast and sinkers may not hold bottom properly.
